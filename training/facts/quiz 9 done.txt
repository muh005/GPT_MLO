Question 1:
Question:
What two early disclosures are integrated into one disclosure?
Answer (Correct): Good Faith Estimate and the early TIL disclosure
Feedback: TRID established the basis for forming the Loan Estimate by combining the Good Faith Estimate
and the early TIL disclosure.
Question 2:
Question:
Which transaction is not covered under the TILA-RESPA Rule?
Answer (Correct): Reverse mortgages
Feedback: Reverse mortgage are covered under RESPA and not the TILA-RESPA rule.
Question 3:
Question:
The initial Loan Estimate must be delivered by mail at least ___ business days before consummation.
Answer (Correct): 7 business days
Feedback: A borrower cannot close on a loan earlier than 7 business day before consummation.

Question 5:
Question:
If there is a change of circumstances requiring a revised Loan Estimate, when must the creditor provide the
Loan Estimate to the applicant?
Answer (Correct): Within three business days of becoming aware of the change
Feedback: Once a creditor uncovers a change circumstance, a revised Loan Estimate must be provided by
the third business day after the creditor becomes aware of the change.
Question 6:
Question:
If an applicant receives the closing disclosure (CD) three business days prior to consummation, when can
they request to see it again?
Answer (Correct): They can request a copy one day before consummation
Feedback: A borrower can request a copy of the closing disclosure one day before consummation.
Question 7:
Question:
If the Loan Estimate is not delivered in person, but is sent electronically, when is it assumed to be delivered?
Answer (Correct): Three business days later
Feedback: This is unless the borrower can prove they received it earlier.
Question 8:
Question:
When can a revised Loan Estimate be delivered after the Closing Disclosure has been delivered?
Answer (Correct): Never
Feedback: A creditor cannot provide a revised Loan Estimate once a Closing Disclosure is provided.
Question 9:
Question:
A borrower plans to close on the purchase of her home in a few days and would like to review her closing
costs in advance of the settlement. When must the applicant receive the Closing Disclosure?
Answer (Correct): At least 3 business days before consummation
Feedback: Borrowers are required to receive the Closing Disclosure at least 3 business days before signing
the note otherwise referred to as consummation.

Question 10:
Question:
A revised Loan Estimate must be delivered no later than ___ days before consummation, if delivered in
person, or ____ days before consummation, if mailed.
Answer (Correct): 4,7
