Question:
When is a processor required to meet the licensing requirements of a mortgage loan originator?
Answer (Correct): If the processor works on behalf of multiple entities
Feedback: A processor who works on behalf multiple entities is a contract processor and is required to be
registered and/or licensed with the NMLS.

Question 10:
Question:
A processor would be looking for ________ months of bank statements as part of any asset verification.
Answer (Correct): 2
Feedback: A lender requires two (2) months of bank statements. In addition, money must be in a borrower's
bank account for 60 days to be considered seasoned money. Seasoning money refers to the concept of
keeping money in an established bank account for a specific period of time, generally 60 days.
